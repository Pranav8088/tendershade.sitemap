About Tender Shade

Simplifying Government Procurement for Indian Businesses

Our Story

Tender Shade was founded to help Indian businesses streamline their access to government procurement opportunities. With years of domain experience, our team specializes in registering and managing tender profiles across GeM, CPPP, IRPES, and 25+ other portals.

Our mission is to remove the complexities of government bidding and enable every eligible MSME, startup, or enterprise to confidently participate and win tenders.

Our approach is rooted in:

  • In-depth portal knowledge
  • Personalized consultation
  • Fast turnaround time
  • Transparent communication
