Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you complete my GeM registration?

Typically, we can complete the GeM registration process within 72 hours, provided all your documents are in order and ready for submission.

What documents do I need for portal registration?

The basic documents typically include company registration proof, PAN card, GST certificate, bank account details, and address proof. Additional documents may be required based on your business type and the specific portal.

Do you provide ongoing support after completing registration?

Yes, we offer various support packages ranging from basic technical support to complete account management services. Our team can help with catalog management, bid identification, preparation, and submission.

What are your fees for tender management services?

Our fees vary based on the specific services you require. We offer customized packages tailored to your business needs. Please contact us for a personalized quote based on your requirements.
