What is GeM?

The Government e-Marketplace (GeM) is an online procurement platform created to facilitate transparent, efficient, and technology-driven procurement by government departments, PSUs, and other organizations. Sellers can list their products and services, and buyers can directly place orders or initiate bidding processes.

Why GeM?

  • It is now a mandatory procurement platform for most central and state government agencies.
  • Over 50,000+ buying organizations and 10 lakh+ sellers use the portal.
  • All procurement above INR 25,000 must be routed via GeM.

GeM for Startups

  • Special policies to support startups
  • Relaxed eligibility criteria
  • Exemption from past performance requirements
  • Recognition via DPIIT certificate
  • Direct purchase preference for innovative products/services
  • Significantly easier for startups to break into government procurement

Vendor Assessment (VA)

We help sellers prepare and complete the mandatory Vendor Assessment process which validates your capacity and capability to deliver quality products/services. For some categories, vendor assessment is critical to satisfy buyer requirements and enhance trust. However, navigating the documentation, pre-assessment, and compliance steps can be time-consuming.

  • Post-Assessment Support & Compliance Advisory: Report clarifications, post-assessment guidance, compliance tracking
  • Vendor Assessment Exemption Support: Evaluation for DPIIT, NSIC, NABL, and other exemptions; documentation and application support
