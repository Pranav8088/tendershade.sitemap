Frequently Asked Questions

What makes professional account management better than managing it ourselves?

Professional management brings specialized expertise, dedicated resources, platform-specific knowledge, and continuous attention that internal teams often cannot provide. Our experts stay updated on all GeM changes, apply best practices, and leverage data-driven strategies to maximize your success.

How quickly will I see results from your account management services?

Most clients begin seeing improvements in visibility and engagement within 2-4 weeks. Significant increases in bid opportunities and conversions typically occur within 1-3 months, depending on your product categories and initial account condition.

Can you handle multiple product categories and large catalogs?

Yes, we have experience managing accounts with diverse product portfolios and extensive catalogs across various government procurement categories. Our team has the bandwidth and expertise to handle comprehensive catalog management regardless of size or complexity.

What level of involvement is required from our team?

While we handle the day-to-day management, we'll need some initial input regarding your products, capabilities, and business goals. We'll also require occasional feedback on bid opportunities and order fulfillment coordination. Beyond that, our goal is to minimize your team's involvement while keeping you informed through regular reporting.
